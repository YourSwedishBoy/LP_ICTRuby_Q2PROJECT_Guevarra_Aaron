Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh ground pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk together eggs and Parmesan.
  4. Combine pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture. Serve with fresh pepper.
