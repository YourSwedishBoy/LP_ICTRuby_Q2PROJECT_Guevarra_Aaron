Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 poached egg
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it evenly on the toast.
  3. Top with a poached egg and season with salt and pepper.
