Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 200g grilled chicken
  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken and slice it thinly.
  2. Tear the lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Combine chicken, lettuce, croutons, and Caesar dressing.
  4. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
